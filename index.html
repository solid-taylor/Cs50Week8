Travel is my passion

It doesn't matter travelling by car or plane, or ship, or even by bare foot, the journey is more important then the destination.

I'm lucky having the free time and the opputunities to travel at least 5-6 times a year, and having my partner/best friend/wife to share this passion with.

For 4 years now we have a new travel mate, who sees the world with his eyes wide open, because he sees everything for the first time. He is our wonderfull son of course.

So this trio is our team. I wish everyone have the oppurtunities to see different places, different cultures. Because travelling makes you better.

Beyond the allways-said "it widens your perspective"-thing, you also get to know yourself better.

You get used to the uncertainty as well, which is a usefull skill in everyday life. When you travel, you never can be sure what happens next, and there lies the fun part.
